
QUE 4. Get department wise average salary from employee table order by salary ascending

mysql> SELECT department, AVG(salary) Average_Salary
    -> FROM employees
    -> GROUP BY department
    -> ORDER BY Average_Salary ASC;

| department | Average_Salary |
+------------+----------------+
